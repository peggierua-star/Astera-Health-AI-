About Astera Health AI

Astera Health AI is a healthcare technology company focused on reducing paperwork and improving how patient information is organized and accessed.

The platform is designed for healthcare organizations that want a cleaner, faster, and more reliable way to manage administrative work. Instead of scattered paperwork and manual follow-ups, teams get a more organized system that supports daily operations.
